  1. Explanation: Know why a decision was made
  2. Transparency: Understand the AI system and how it works
  3. Appeal: Request review by different human within 30 days
  4. Human Review: Request decision from human only, without AI
  5. Data Protection: Know what data was used; have it protected
  6. Fair Treatment: Assessment based on financial capacity, not identity
  7. Privacy: Personal information kept confidential
  8. Remedy: Seek correction and compensation if wronged
